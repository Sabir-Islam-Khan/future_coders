About the Event

FutureCoders Bootcamp is a two-day immersive tech event combining expert-led talks, hands-on coding sessions, and a collaborative environment surrounded by nature.

Join us in the scenic hills of Sreemongol for an unforgettable experience where technology meets nature. Network with fellow developers, learn from industry experts, and enhance your coding skills in one of Bangladesh's most beautiful locations.

Whether you're a student looking to kickstart your tech career or an experienced developer wanting to expand your knowledge, this bootcamp offers something for everyone.
